# Block F: Knowledge Development and AI Architecture

## Purpose
Description of the universal mechanism for the development of any intelligent system. The block describes the transition from changing reality to changing architecture through the accumulation of knowledge.

## Reality (R)
Reality is the constantly changing environment of the architecture's existence.

## Change in Reality (ΔR)
Formula: ΔR → P

## Problem (P)
The mismatch between the existing architecture and the new reality.

## Pain (B)
Formula: P → B

## Impact (S)
Any external event that changes the state of the system.

## Reaction (Re)
Formula: S → Re

## Attention (W)
Attention is measured by the system's reaction to the impact. Formula: Re → W

## Hypothesis (H)
Formula: W → H

## New Knowledge (N)
Formula: H → N

## Architecture Change
Formula: N → ΔЖ

## Quality of Architecture (K)
Research hypothesis: K(t), dK/dt, d²K/dt²

## Artificial Intelligence (AI)
A tool for analysis, hypothesis generation, and supporting the change of knowledge architecture.

## Main Idea
ΔR → P → B → S → Re → W → H → N → ΔЖ

## Main Goal
To create a universal model of knowledge development applicable to both humans and artificial intelligence.

## Main Question of the Architect
How should an intelligent system change its own architecture to constantly reduce the gap from changing reality?
