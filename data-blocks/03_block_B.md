# Block B: Calculations and Coefficients

## Main Task
Transformation of architectural solutions into measurable values.

## 1. Cost
Designation: C
C = Cmoney + Ctime + Crisk + Closses
where:
- Cmoney — direct expenses
- Ctime — cost of time
- Crisk — cost of risk
- Closses — cost of consequences

## 2. Time
Designation: T
There is no cost without time.

## 3. Velocity
Designation: V
V = dX/dT
The rate of change of any value.

## 4. Acceleration
A = d²X/dT²

## 5. Risk
Designation: R
Risk must have a monetary equivalent.

## 6. Integral
Accumulated effect:
∫P(t)dt
or
∫C(t)dt

## 7. Architectural Intersection Coefficient
Ka
Ka = 0 — architectures do not intersect.
Ka = 1 — fully coincide.
Ka > 0.8 — high probability of conflict.

## 8. Benefit
G = Income − Cost

## 9. Main Formula
Gc > Cf
Gc — benefit of cooperation.
Cf — cost of conflict.
