# Block E: New Patents and Research Directions

## Purpose
Formation of a portfolio of future architectural solutions, research, technologies, and patents. The main task is to ensure continuous architectural development through research, experiments, and the creation of new knowledge.

## Research
Designation: Rs
A purposeful process of obtaining new knowledge.

## Experiment
Designation: Ex
Practical verification of hypotheses. An experiment can confirm a hypothesis, refute it, or generate a new one.

## Architectural Idea
Designation: Ai
A new architectural solution with potential added value.

## Architectural Desert
Uses results from Block C. The most promising areas are those with minimal architectural density.

## Artificial Intelligence
Designation: AI
AI is a research tool: generating hypotheses, analyzing information, finding patterns, modeling scenarios, processing large amounts of data, searching for new architectural solutions, and assisting the architect in decision-making. AI expands the architect's capabilities but does not replace them.

## Patent Strategy
Each new patent must reinforce previous ones. The patent portfolio is viewed as a single architectural system.

## Main Principle
Each new patent must protect the system's architecture, not an individual detail.

## Main Goal
To create a consistent system of interconnected architectural solutions that ensure long-term technological leadership.

## Main Question of the Architect
What new architectural solution is capable of changing the development of the entire system, rather than just improving one of its elements?
